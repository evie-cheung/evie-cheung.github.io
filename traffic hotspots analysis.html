Traffic Accident Hotspot Analysis

Project Type: Master’s Research Paper

Description: Conducted a detailed spatial analysis to identify traffic accident hotspots using Kernel Density Estimation and Moran’s I spatial autocorrelation techniques. The analysis aimed to support evidence-based transport policy and safety decisions.

Technologies Used: Spatial analysis methods (KDE, Moran’s I), GIS tools

Outcome: Delivered actionable insights for accident hotspot prioritisation, supporting policy and strategic planning.
